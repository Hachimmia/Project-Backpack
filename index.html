About the backpack

Your backpack. Exactly how you want it.

It's Practical

We designed this backpack with practicality and ease of use in mind. It doesn't matter how you have your backpack configured, it will stay easy to use.

High Capacity

with the basic capacity holding 40 Litres, you will be able to bring all the essentials with you where ever you need to go.

Complete security

When you purchase a backpack, you also get a guaranteed 10 year warranty. We believe in the quality of our products but if it falls short within the expected lifetime we will give your backpack a refurbishment or a replacement free of charge.

Pure quality

Sturdy, Resilient and Eco-friendly. We use Recycled Oxford Polyester with a PU Coating for added water resistance

Completely Customisable

Need to go to the gym? there's a configuration for that. Want to go hiking? there's a configuration for that. Just need to go to the library to study? there's a configuration for that. We pride ourselves on the versatility of this backpack, there is no inherent purpose for the extensions to the backpack. It is completely up to you how you use this backpack and its features
